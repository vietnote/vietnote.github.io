Introduction

Neural networks are a subset of machine learning and are at the heart of deep learning algorithms. Their structure is inspired by the human brain, containing layers of interconnected neurons[1]Inspired by the brain's structure.

Basic Concepts

A neural network consists of input layers, hidden layers, and output layers. Each neuron in one layer is connected to neurons in the next layer with associated weights[Smith, 2020]Smith, J. "Introduction to Neural Networks", 2020..
